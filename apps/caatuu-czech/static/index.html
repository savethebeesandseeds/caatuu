Small scripts

Read a dictionary item

město town, city N n>město
Jdu do města. do+G: města
  1. Top left: Czech word to recognize or say.
  2. Top middle: red translation, so you can scan fast.
  3. Top right: type code. For nouns, > points to the ending model.
  4. Bottom left: copyable sentence.
  5. Bottom right: grammar cue that explains the sentence.

Cases are operations

N G D A L I

A cue such as do+G: města says which case the sentence needs and gives the real form to copy. The dictionary uses six working cases; vocative is for calling someone and is not part of the row system.
