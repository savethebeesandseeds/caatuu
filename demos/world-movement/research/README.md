# World Movement research workspace

This directory records prompts, decisions, and small review artifacts for the
World Movement Lab. High-volume processing areas intentionally remain local:

- `source/` — downloaded vendor archives and extracted source libraries;
- `generated-candidates/` — image-generation outputs and processing previews.
- `conditioning/`, `keypose-authority/`, and `silhouettes/` — intermediate
  pose guides, masks, cards, split frames, and review previews.

The demo tracks only the curated frames and manifests required by its browser
surfaces. A third-party motion asset promoted into `motion-reference/source/`
must include its exact source and license record beside the asset.
