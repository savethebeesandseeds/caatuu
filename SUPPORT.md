# Caatuu support

Caatuu is a free development preview maintained on a best-effort basis. It does
not currently include paid support, guaranteed response times, or a supported
stable release.

- Ordinary reproducible bugs may be reported through the public GitHub issue
  tracker without personal or sensitive data.
- Security, privacy, and account-sensitive questions should be sent privately
  to `contact@waajacu.com`.
- Caatuu is a language-learning tool, not professional, medical, legal,
  financial, or safety advice.

External code, model, dataset, and artwork contributions remain paused under
[`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md). A future paid pilot or support offer must
have written scope, price, delivery terms, operator identity, and invoicing
process before it is advertised.
